04
Map-first · Spatial · Civic gravitas

Atlas

The dashboard is an aerial view of Amman, with applications represented as glowing pins at their actual lot locations. The reviewer screen splits between satellite imagery and the CAD drawing georeferenced on top. The platform anchored to the city it serves.

  • Dashboard = stylized aerial of Amman with verdict pins
  • Cormorant serif + JetBrains Mono
  • Compass roses, scale bars, coordinate readouts
  • Best when civic stature matters

05
Drafting sheet · Domain-specific · Memorable

Blueprint

The entire app is rendered as an architect's drafting sheet. Title block in the corner, dimension callouts with leader lines, a "schedule of dimensions" in place of a discrepancy table, red-pen reviewer markup, and rubber stamps for approve/reject. Pays homage to the medium being reviewed.

  • Cyanotype blueprint background · graph-paper grid
  • Special Elite (typewriter) + Caveat (handwriting) + Permanent Marker (stamps)
  • Drawing index dashboard · sheet review for each application
  • Best for visual identity — distinctive on first sight

06
Brutalist · AI-protagonist · Cinematic

Reactor

The AI is the protagonist. Brutalist oversized typography, terminal-style command surface, live reasoning stream as the hero. The review screen leads with a colossal headline number — the verdict becomes a single, unmissable moment. Made for the Linear / v0 / Cursor era.

  • Pitch black · electric acid green accent
  • Archivo Black + JetBrains Mono · oversized type
  • Command-K palette · streaming agent feed · cinematic verdicts
  • Best for an AI-first product narrative
